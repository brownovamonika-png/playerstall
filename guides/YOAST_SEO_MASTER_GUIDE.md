## SEO Analysis Requirements

### ✅ 1. Focus Keyphrase Requirements

#### Keyphrase Length
- **Minimum:** 1 word
- **Maximum:** 4 words (Yoast recommends 1-3 words)
- **Best Practice:** 2-3 words for optimal balance

#### Keyphrase Distribution
- **Must appear in:**
  1. ✅ SEO Title (at the beginning)
  2. ✅ Meta Description (1-2 times max)
  3. ✅ URL/Slug (exact match or close variation)
  4. ✅ H1 Heading (first heading)
  5. ✅ Introduction (first 100-150 words)
  6. ✅ Body Content (13-20 times for 3,000-word post)
  7. ✅ Subheadings (H2/H3) - 30-50% of subheadings should contain keyphrase
  8. ✅ Image Alt Text (at least 1 image)
  9. ✅ Conclusion (last paragraph)

#### Keyphrase Density Formula
```
Keyphrase Density = (Number of Keyphrase Instances / Total Word Count) × 100
```

**Optimal Range:**
- **Minimum:** 0.5% (for 3,000 words = 15 instances)
- **Maximum:** 2.5% (for 3,000 words = 75 instances)
- **Sweet Spot:** 1-1.5% (for 3,000 words = 30-45 instances)

**Yoast Requirements by Word Count:**
- 1,000 words: 5-10 instances
- 2,000 words: 10-20 instances
- 3,000 words: 15-30 instances
- 4,000 words: 20-40 instances

**Calculation Example:**
- Post: 3,200 words
- Keyphrase: "football locker room design"
- Minimum needed: 13 instances
- Maximum allowed: 80 instances
- Optimal: 20-30 instances

#### Keyphrase in Subheadings
- **Target:** 30-50% of H2/H3 headings contain keyphrase
- **Too Low:** <20% (Yoast will flag)
- **Too High:** >75% (over-optimization warning)
- **Best Practice:** Include in 4-6 out of 10 subheadings

### ✅ 2. SEO Title Requirements

#### Length
- **Minimum:** 30 characters
- **Maximum:** 60 characters (Yoast shows preview at 60)
- **Optimal:** 50-60 characters
- **Warning:** >60 characters (will be truncated in search results)

#### Format
```
[Keyphrase] | [Benefit/Year] | [Brand]
```

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Football Locker Room Design Guide | PlayerStall" (47 chars)
- ✅ "College Sports Lockers: 2025 Buyer's Guide | PlayerStall" (55 chars)
- ❌ "Football Locker Room Design: Complete Planning Guide for Athletic Directors | PlayerStall" (78 chars - TOO LONG)

#### Keyphrase Position
- **Must be:** At the beginning of SEO title
- **Why:** Higher click-through rates, better SEO
- **Format:** `[Keyphrase] | [Additional Info] | [Brand]`

---

### ✅ 3. Meta Description Requirements

#### Length
- **Minimum:** 120 characters
- **Maximum:** 160 characters (Yoast shows preview at 160)
- **Optimal:** 150-160 characters
- **Warning:** <120 or >160 characters

#### Keyphrase Usage
- **Must contain:** Focus keyphrase 1-2 times
- **Maximum:** 2 times (Yoast flags if >2)
- **Position:** Preferably in first 120 characters

#### Content Requirements
- ✅ Include primary benefit or value proposition
- ✅ Include call-to-action (CTA)
- ✅ Include unique selling point (30+ years, lifetime guarantee, etc.)
- ✅ Make it compelling and click-worthy

**Formula:**
```
[Keyphrase] + [Benefit] + [CTA/USP] = 150-160 characters
```

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Complete planning guide for athletic directors: equipment storage, team branding, space planning, and recruiting impact. 30+ years experience." (156 chars)
- ❌ "Complete guide to football locker room design: equipment storage, team branding, space planning, and recruiting impact. 30+ years experience. Free consultation available." (178 chars - TOO LONG)

---

### ✅ 4. URL/Slug Requirements

#### Format
- **Must contain:** Focus keyphrase (exact or close variation)
- **Format:** kebab-case (lowercase, hyphens)
- **Length:** 3-5 words optimal
- **Avoid:** Stop words (a, an, the, and, or, but)

**Examples:**
- ✅ `football-locker-room-design-guide`
- ✅ `college-sports-lockers-guide`
- ❌ `blog-post-4` (no keyphrase)
- ❌ `football_locker_room_design` (underscores, not hyphens)

---

### ✅ 5. H1 Heading Requirements

#### Content
- **Must contain:** Focus keyphrase
- **Position:** First heading in content
- **Format:** Should match or closely match SEO title
- **Length:** 50-70 characters optimal

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Football Locker Room Design: Complete Planning Guide for Athletic Directors"
- ✅ "College Sports Lockers: 2025 Buyer's Guide"

---

### ✅ 6. Introduction Requirements

#### Keyphrase Placement
- **Must appear:** In first paragraph (first 100-150 words)
- **Position:** Within first 2-3 sentences ideal
- **Natural:** Should flow naturally, not forced

**Example:**
```
After 30+ years designing locker facilities for football programs, 
we've learned what separates great football locker room design from 
good ones. [Keyphrase appears naturally in first paragraph]
```

---

### ✅ 7. Internal Links Requirements

#### Minimum
- **Required:** At least 1 internal link
- **Optimal:** 3-5 internal links for 3,000-word post
- **Best Practice:** 1 internal link per 500-800 words

#### Link Quality
- ✅ Link to relevant pages (product pages, service pages, related blog posts)
- ✅ Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- ✅ Include keyphrase or related terms in anchor text when natural
- ✅ Link to high-value pages (contact, services, products)

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Learn more about our [custom football lockers](/products)"
- ✅ "See our [college sports locker solutions](/products)"
- ❌ "Click here for more info" (not descriptive)

---

### ✅ 8. External Links Requirements

#### Minimum
- **Required:** At least 1 external link
- **Optimal:** 2-3 external links for 3,000-word post
- **Best Practice:** Link to authoritative sources

#### Link Quality
- ✅ Link to authoritative, relevant sources
- ✅ Use `target="_blank" rel="noopener"` for external links
- ✅ Link to reputable organizations (NCAA, sports associations, etc.)
- ✅ Link adds value to reader

**Examples:**
- ✅ Link to NCAA.org for compliance information
- ✅ Link to sports facility associations
- ✅ Link to research studies or industry reports
- ❌ Link to competitors
- ❌ Link to low-quality sites

---

### ✅ 9. Image Requirements

#### Minimum
- **Required:** At least 1 image
- **Optimal:** 3-5 images for 3,000-word post
- **Best Practice:** 1 image per 500-800 words

#### Image Alt Text Requirements
- **Must contain:** Focus keyphrase in at least 1 image
- **Format:** Descriptive, natural language
- **Length:** 5-15 words optimal
- **All images:** Must have alt text (accessibility requirement)

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Football locker room design with custom wood lockers showing proper equipment storage and team branding"
- ✅ "College sports lockers with team branding and player name engraving"
- ❌ "Image 1" (not descriptive)
- ❌ "locker" (too short, missing keyphrase)

#### Image Placement
- ✅ First image within first 300 words
- ✅ Images distributed throughout content
- ✅ Images break up long text sections
- ✅ Images relevant to surrounding content

---

### ✅ 10. Keyphrase Distribution

#### Even Distribution
- **Requirement:** Keyphrase should appear throughout content, not just at beginning
- **Check:** No large gaps (>500 words) without keyphrase
- **Method:** Spread instances evenly across all sections

**Distribution Pattern:**
- Introduction: 1-2 instances
- Section 1: 2-3 instances
- Section 2: 2-3 instances
- Section 3: 2-3 instances
- Section 4: 2-3 instances
- Conclusion: 1-2 instances

## Readability Analysis Requirements

### ✅ 1. Flesch Reading Ease Score

#### Target Score
- **Minimum:** 60 (8th-9th grade reading level)
- **Optimal:** 60-70 (easy to read)
- **Warning:** <60 (too difficult)
- **Too Easy:** >80 (may seem too simple)

#### How to Improve
- ✅ Use shorter sentences (average 15-20 words)
- ✅ Use simpler words when possible
- ✅ Break up complex ideas
- ✅ Use active voice
- ✅ Add examples and explanations

---

### ✅ 2. Sentence Length

#### Requirements
- **Maximum:** 25% of sentences can exceed 20 words
- **Optimal:** Average 15-20 words per sentence
- **Warning:** >25% of sentences exceed 20 words

#### How to Check
```
Count sentences over 20 words
Divide by total sentences
Multiply by 100
Result should be <25%
```

**Example:**
- Total sentences: 100
- Sentences >20 words: 20
- Percentage: 20% ✅ (under 25%)

---

### ✅ 3. Paragraph Length

#### Requirements
- **Optimal:** 2-4 sentences per paragraph
- **Maximum:** 6 sentences per paragraph
- **Warning:** Paragraphs with >6 sentences

#### Best Practice
- ✅ One idea per paragraph
- ✅ 3-5 sentences optimal
- ✅ Break up long paragraphs
- ✅ Use white space effectively

---

### ✅ 4. Subheading Distribution

#### Requirements
- **Maximum:** 300 words between subheadings
- **Optimal:** Subheading every 200-300 words
- **Warning:** Sections with >300 words without subheading

#### How to Check
```
Count words between H2/H3 headings
If any section >300 words, add subheading
```

**Example:**
- Section 1: 250 words ✅
- Section 2: 450 words ❌ (add subheading)
- Section 3: 180 words ✅

---

### ✅ 5. Passive Voice

#### Requirements
- **Maximum:** 10% of sentences can be passive voice
- **Optimal:** <5% passive voice
- **Warning:** >10% passive voice

#### How to Identify Passive Voice
- ✅ Active: "We design custom lockers"
- ❌ Passive: "Custom lockers are designed by us"

#### How to Fix
- ✅ Use active voice: "We do X" instead of "X is done"
- ✅ Make subject perform the action
- ✅ Use strong verbs

---

### ✅ 6. Transition Words

#### Requirements
- **Minimum:** 30% of sentences should contain transition words
- **Optimal:** 30-40% of sentences
- **Warning:** <30% transition words

#### Common Transition Words
- **Addition:** Additionally, Furthermore, Moreover, Also, In addition
- **Contrast:** However, But, Although, Nevertheless, On the other hand
- **Cause/Effect:** Therefore, Thus, Consequently, As a result, Because
- **Time:** First, Then, Next, Finally, Meanwhile, Afterward
- **Example:** For example, For instance, Specifically, In particular
- **Summary:** In conclusion, To summarize, Overall, In summary

#### How to Check
```
Count sentences with transition words
Divide by total sentences
Multiply by 100
Result should be ≥30%
```

**Example:**
- Total sentences: 100
- Sentences with transitions: 35
- Percentage: 35% ✅ (above 30%)

---

### ✅ 7. Consecutive Sentences

#### Requirements
- **Maximum:** No more than 2 consecutive sentences starting with same word
- **Warning:** 3+ consecutive sentences starting with same word
- **Optimal:** Vary sentence starters throughout

#### How to Fix
- ✅ Vary sentence beginnings
- ✅ Use different words: "We", "They", "Additionally", "However"
- ✅ Start with transition words
- ✅ Use different sentence structures

**Example:**
- ❌ "We design lockers. We build them. We install them."
- ✅ "We design lockers. Our team builds them. Additionally, we handle installation."

---

### ✅ 8. Lists & Bullet Points

#### Requirements
- **Use lists:** When presenting 3+ items
- **Format:** Use proper HTML list tags (`<ul>`, `<ol>`)
- **Benefit:** Improves readability, breaks up text

#### When to Use Lists
- ✅ Key takeaways
- ✅ Features or benefits
- ✅ Steps in a process
- ✅ Multiple examples
- ✅ Comparison points

## Exact Formulas & Thresholds

### Keyphrase Density Calculator

```
For 3,000-word post:
- Minimum instances: 15 (0.5% density)
- Maximum instances: 75 (2.5% density)
- Optimal instances: 30-45 (1-1.5% density)

Formula: (Instances / Word Count) × 100 = Density %
```

### SEO Title Length Checker

```
Character count (including spaces):
- 30-60: ✅ Green
- 60-70: ⚠️ Yellow (will be truncated)
- >70: ❌ Red (too long)
```

### Meta Description Length Checker

```
Character count (including spaces):
- 120-160: ✅ Green
- 100-120: ⚠️ Yellow (too short)
- 160-180: ⚠️ Yellow (will be truncated)
- >180: ❌ Red (too long)
```

### Transition Words Percentage

```
(Sentences with transitions / Total sentences) × 100
- ≥30%: ✅ Green
- 20-30%: ⚠️ Yellow
- <20%: ❌ Red
```

### Subheading Distribution

```
Words between subheadings:
- <300 words: ✅ Green
- 300-400 words: ⚠️ Yellow
- >400 words: ❌ Red (add subheading)
```

## Pre-Writing Checklist

Before you start writing, complete this checklist:

### Keyword Research
- [ ] Primary keyphrase selected (2-3 words, 30-200 monthly searches)
- [ ] Keyphrase length: 1-4 words
- [ ] Competition level checked (low-medium ideal)
- [ ] Related keyphrases identified (3-5 terms)
- [ ] Synonyms identified (5-10 terms)

### Content Planning
- [ ] Target word count: 3,000+ words
- [ ] Outline created with 5-8 main sections
- [ ] Subheadings planned (every 200-300 words)
- [ ] Keyphrase placement planned:
  - [ ] In SEO title (beginning)
  - [ ] In meta description (1-2 times)
  - [ ] In URL/slug
  - [ ] In H1 heading
  - [ ] In introduction (first paragraph)
  - [ ] In 30-50% of subheadings
  - [ ] Throughout body (15-30 instances)
  - [ ] In conclusion

### Link Planning
- [ ] 3-5 internal links identified (to product/service pages)
- [ ] 2-3 external links identified (authoritative sources)
- [ ] Anchor text planned (descriptive, natural)

### Image Planning
- [ ] 3-5 images identified/planned
- [ ] Alt text planned (includes keyphrase for at least 1 image)
- [ ] Image placement planned (first image within 300 words)

## Writing Template

Use this template structure for every blog post:

```html
<!-- TITLE (for WordPress) -->
<h1>[Keyphrase]: [Benefit/Year] | [Brand]</h1>

<!-- META DESCRIPTION -->
<!-- Meta Description: [150-160 chars: Keyphrase + Benefit + CTA/USP] -->

<!-- FOCUS KEYWORD -->
<!-- Focus Keyword: [your keyphrase] -->

<!-- INTRODUCTION (300-400 words) -->
<p><strong>[Hook with keyphrase in first 2-3 sentences]</strong></p>
<p><strong>[Problem statement]</strong></p>
<p><strong>[What they'll learn - include keyphrase]</strong></p>
<p><strong>[Value proposition - 30+ years, etc.]</strong></p>

<!-- KEY TAKEAWAYS -->
<h2>Key Takeaways</h2>
<ul>
<li>[Takeaway 1]</li>
<li>[Takeaway 2]</li>
<li>[Takeaway 3]</li>
<li>[Takeaway 4]</li>
<li>[Takeaway 5]</li>
</ul>

<!-- TABLE OF CONTENTS (if 2,500+ words) -->
<h2>Quick Navigation</h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="#section1">[Section 1]</a></li>
<li><a href="#section2">[Section 2]</a></li>
<!-- etc. -->
</ul>

<!-- IMAGE 1 (within first 300 words) -->
<img src="[URL]" alt="[Keyphrase] [descriptive text]" />

<!-- MAIN SECTION 1 (400-500 words) -->
<h2 id="section1">[Keyphrase in H2 or synonym]</h2>
<p>[Content with keyphrase 2-3 times naturally]</p>

<h3>[Subsection - keyphrase or synonym]</h3>
<p>[Content with transition words]</p>

<!-- MAIN SECTION 2 (400-500 words) -->
<h2 id="section2">[Keyphrase in H2 or synonym]</h2>
<p>[Content with keyphrase 2-3 times]</p>

<!-- IMAGE 2 (mid-content) -->
<img src="[URL]" alt="[Keyphrase] [descriptive text]" />

<!-- Continue sections... -->

<!-- FAQ SECTION -->
<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>
<h3>[Question with keyphrase]</h3>
<p>[Answer]</p>
<!-- Repeat 6-8 times -->

<!-- CONCLUSION -->
<h2>Conclusion</h2>
<p>[Summary with keyphrase 1-2 times]</p>
<p><strong>[CTA with internal link]</strong></p>

<!-- INTERNAL LINKS -->
<p>Learn more about our <a href="/products">[related service]</a></p>

<!-- EXTERNAL LINKS -->
<p>According to <a href="[authoritative source]" target="_blank" rel="noopener">[Source Name]</a>, [relevant information]</p>
```

## Common Mistakes & Solutions

### Mistake 1: Keyphrase Over-Optimization
**Problem:** Keyphrase appears 100+ times (density >2.5%)
**Solution:** Replace some instances with synonyms
**Check:** Use synonyms in 50-60% of instances

### Mistake 2: Keyphrase Under-Optimization
**Problem:** Keyphrase appears only 2-5 times
**Solution:** Add keyphrase naturally throughout content
**Target:** 15-30 instances for 3,000-word post

### Mistake 3: Meta Description Too Long
**Problem:** Meta description 180+ characters
**Solution:** Cut to 150-160 characters, remove filler words
**Formula:** Keyphrase + Benefit + CTA = 150-160 chars

### Mistake 4: SEO Title Too Long
**Problem:** SEO title 70+ characters
**Solution:** Shorten to 50-60 characters, remove unnecessary words
**Format:** [Keyphrase] | [Benefit] | [Brand]

### Mistake 5: Missing Transition Words
**Problem:** Only 10-15% of sentences have transitions
**Solution:** Add transition words to 30%+ of sentences
**List:** Use provided transition word list

### Mistake 6: Consecutive Sentence Starts
**Problem:** 5+ sentences start with same word
**Solution:** Vary sentence starters, use transitions
**Check:** No more than 2 consecutive sentences with same start

### Mistake 7: Long Sections Without Subheadings
**Problem:** 500+ word sections without subheadings
**Solution:** Add H2/H3 subheadings every 200-300 words
**Check:** Break up any section >300 words

### Mistake 8: Missing Images
**Problem:** No images or images without alt text
**Solution:** Add 3-5 images with descriptive alt text
**Requirement:** At least 1 alt text contains keyphrase

### Mistake 9: No External Links
**Problem:** Only internal links, no external authority links
**Solution:** Add 2-3 links to authoritative sources
**Sources:** NCAA, sports associations, research studies

### Mistake 10: Keyphrase Not in Introduction
**Problem:** Keyphrase appears first time in paragraph 3+
**Solution:** Include keyphrase in first paragraph (first 100-150 words)
**Position:** Within first 2-3 sentences ideal
