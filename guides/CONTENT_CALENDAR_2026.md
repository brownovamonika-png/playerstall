## Content Standards (Every Post)

- **Title:** Primary keyword in first 60 chars
- **Description:** ≤160 chars, keyword + benefit
- **FAQs:** 6–8 Q&A pairs in frontmatter (auto-generates FAQPage JSON-LD schema)
- **Word count:** 2,100–3,200
- **Internal links:** Minimum 5 per post
- **CTA:** Always ends with consultation CTA + 30-year experience mention

## Internal Linking Architecture
- Jul 1 pillar receives links from every prior post
- Apr 8 hub ("High School Athletic Lockers") anchors all 7 state posts
- Apr 28 (Budget Approval) ↔ May 20 (Pricing Guide) cross-link to sport/state posts
- Apr 22 (Women's Sports) cross-links to lacrosse, soccer, basketball, volleyball posts

## Blog Writing Skills
Two custom skills are available in this repo for drafting and auditing posts:
- `/blog-draft [title] [keyword]` — drafts a complete MDX post, reads all /guides/ research first
- `/blog-seo-audit [file-path]` — scores a post 0–50 across 5 SEO dimensions, returns specific fixes
