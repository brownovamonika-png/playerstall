## Key Improvements Made

### 1. Structure & Organization
- **Before:** Pricing first, then information
- **After:** Acknowledgment → Value Props → Quote → Next Steps
- **Benefit:** More consultative, less transactional

### 2. Value Proposition Emphasis
- **Added:** "30+ years experience" (key differentiator)
- **Added:** "Canadian-owned and operated since 1996" (quality differentiator)
- **Added:** "Free Design Consultation" (competitive advantage)
- **Benefit:** Highlights what makes PlayerStall unique

### 3. Professional Signature
- **Before:** Simple name and contact
- **After:** Full signature block with title and key differentiators
- **Benefit:** More professional, reinforces brand

### 4. Consultative Language
- **Before:** "If you could send me..."
- **After:** "I'd love to see..." / "Let's schedule a call..."
- **Benefit:** Partnership approach vs. request

### 5. Timeline Expectations
- **Added:** "Quote valid for 60 days"
- **Added:** "15-minute call" (specific, not intimidating)
- **Benefit:** Sets clear expectations

### 6. Next Steps Clarity
- **Before:** Implied next step
- **After:** Numbered, clear action items
- **Benefit:** Easier for customer to know what to do
