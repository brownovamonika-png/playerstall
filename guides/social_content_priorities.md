# Social Content Priorities
## Low-Effort, High-Impact Topics for Immediate Creation

Based on market gaps analysis and brand voice, here are prioritized social content topics that can be co-created today.

---

## TIER 1: QUICK WINS (Start Today)

### 1. **"30 Years of Experience" Quick Tips Series**
**Market Gap**: Buyer Journey Support, Post-Sale Support  
**Effort**: ⭐ Low | **Impact**: ⭐⭐⭐ High

**Content Ideas:**
- **"Curt's Quick Tip #1"** - Carousel post with 5-7 tips from 30+ years
  - "Tip: Always measure twice, order once. Here's what I've learned..."
  - "Tip: Wood lockers breathe better than metal. Here's why..."
  - "Tip: Modular design means one broken part doesn't mean a new locker..."
  
- **Format**: Instagram carousel, LinkedIn single image, Facebook post
- **Co-creation**: Curt shares 1-2 tips per day, team creates visuals
- **Hook**: "After 30+ years making wood lockers, here's what I wish every athletic director knew..."

**Why It Works:**
- Leverages unique 30+ years advantage
- Educational (addresses buyer journey gap)
- Authentic voice (Curt's personal tips)
- Low effort (just tips + simple graphics)

  - Slide 2: Durability comparison (wood lasts longer)
  - Slide 3: Cost over 15 years (wood wins on TCO)

  - Slide 5: Maintenance (wood easier to repair)
  - Slide 6: Sustainability (wood = renewable)
  - Slide 7: "The choice is clear" + CTA

- **Format**: Instagram carousel, LinkedIn carousel, Facebook post
- **Co-creation**: Use existing knowledge, create simple comparison graphics
- **Hook**: "Everyone asks: wood or metal? After 30+ years, here's the truth..."

**Why It Works:**
- Addresses core value proposition (wood superiority)
- Educational content (ROI/TCO gap)
- Visual and shareable
- Can be repurposed across platforms
