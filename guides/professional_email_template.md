## When to Use Each Version

**Full Version (Recommended):**
- First-time customers
- Larger orders (50+ lockers)
- When you want to emphasize value and professionalism
- When competing against other quotes

**Concise Version:**
- Repeat customers
- Smaller orders
- When customer is already familiar with your products
- Quick responses needed
