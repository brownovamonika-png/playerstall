## 9. Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks

**Risk 1: Low Individual Page Traffic**
- **Impact**: Individual pages may get <10 searches/month
- **Mitigation**: Focus on aggregate value (100 pages × 10 searches = 1,000 searches)
- **Strategy**: Track aggregate metrics, not individual page performance

**Risk 2: Duplicate Content Penalties**
- **Impact**: Google may penalize similar content across pages
- **Mitigation**: Ensure >80% unique content per page
- **Strategy**: Vary state-specific and sport-specific sections significantly

**Risk 3: Slow Indexing**
- **Impact**: Pages may not index quickly
- **Mitigation**: Submit to Search Console, build internal links, create sitemap
- **Strategy**: Launch in batches (10 pages/week) to avoid overwhelming crawlers

**Risk 4: Low Conversion Rates**
- **Impact**: Traffic may not convert to leads
- **Mitigation**: Strong CTAs, clear value proposition, easy contact methods
- **Strategy**: A/B test CTAs, optimize for conversion

**Risk 5: Template Maintenance Overhead**
- **Impact**: Template updates require updating all pages
- **Mitigation**: Build template with variables, use CMS with template system
- **Strategy**: Plan for template updates, maintain content database
