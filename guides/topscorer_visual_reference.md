# Top Scorer Visual Style Reference

**Reference Website:** https://topscorer.qodeinteractive.com/baseball/

This document serves as a visual style reference for the PlayerStall website. When unsure about style elements, refer to this document and the Top Scorer website.

## Overall Design Philosophy

- **Premium Sports Theme** - Professional, high-quality sports website aesthetic
- **Clean and Modern** - Contemporary design with clear visual hierarchy
- **Athletic Focus** - Sports-oriented imagery and content presentation
- **User-Friendly Navigation** - Clear menu structure and intuitive layout

## Layout Structure

### Header/Navigation
- **Logo Placement**: Main logo centered or left-aligned in header
- **Navigation Menu**: Horizontal menu with dropdowns for sub-pages
- **Menu Items Include**:
  - Home (with sub-pages: Main Home, Baseball, Rugby, Hockey, Landing)
  - Pages (About Us, League Standings, Match Schedule, Our Team, etc.)
  - Sportspress (League, Event, Single Team, Single Player, etc.)
  - Blog (various layouts)
  - Shop (product pages, cart, checkout)
- **Call-to-Action**: "GET TICKETS" button prominently displayed
- **Social Links**: Follow us section in header/footer

### Hero Sections
- **Large Banner Areas**: Full-width hero sections with background images
- **Overlay Text**: Bold headlines with clear call-to-action buttons
- **Multiple Hero Sliders**: Rotating carousel with multiple featured content blocks
- **Typography**: Large, bold headlines (e.g., "Powerful Sports Theme")
- **Action Buttons**: "Read more" and "Contact Us" buttons

### Content Sections

#### Games/Matches Section
- **Section Title**: "WHAT'S TRENDING" or "CHECK OUT THE SCORES"
- **Card Layout**: Grid of match cards showing:
  - Team logos/icons
  - Team names (e.g., "Bears vs Giants")
  - Venue information (e.g., "THE WALLACE STADIUM")
  - Action buttons: "Get Tickets" and "Watch Stream"
- **Visual Style**: Clean cards with team branding

#### Next Match Section
- **Featured Player Card**: Large card with:
  - Player image/photo
  - Position (e.g., "Defensive end")
  - Player name (e.g., "Andrew Salazar")
  - Team information
  - Biography excerpt
  - "View Profile" button

#### Blog/News Section
- **Grid Layout**: Multiple blog post cards
- **Post Cards Include**:
  - Featured image
  - Category tag (e.g., "Season")
  - Title
  - Excerpt
  - "Read More" link
- **Section Title**: "WHAT'S TRENDING" or similar

#### Store/Shop Section
- **Section Title**: "OFFICIAL STORE"
- **Product Grid**: Cards showing:
  - Product image
  - Brand name
  - Product name
  - Price (with strikethrough for sale items)
  - Sale badges ("Sale", "New")
  - "Add to cart" button
- **Visual Indicators**: Sale badges, new badges, sold out indicators

#### Trophy Section
- **Section Title**: "LATEST TEAM TROPHIES"
- **Grid Layout**: Trophy images with:
  - Trophy image
  - Season/year label (e.g., "Season 2019")
  - Category label (e.g., "final four")

#### Features Section
- **Icon-Based Layout**: Icons with titles and descriptions
- **Examples**: Design, Development, Support, WordPress
- **Clean Presentation**: Simple icon + text layout

#### Footer
- **Multi-Column Layout**: Multiple footer sections:
  - About Club (address, contact info)
  - Our Services (list of services)
  - Latest Blog Posts (recent posts with dates)
  - Instagram Feed (social media integration)
- **Contact Information**: Address, phone, email
- **Social Media**: Instagram integration
- **Copyright**: Year and company name

## Typography

### Headlines
- **Large, Bold Headlines**: Used for hero sections and major section titles
- **Examples**: "Powerful Sports Theme", "CHECK OUT THE SCORES", "WHAT'S TRENDING"
- **Style**: Strong, impactful typography

### Section Titles
- **Uppercase Titles**: Many section titles in uppercase (e.g., "GAMES", "WHAT'S TRENDING")
- **Clear Hierarchy**: Different sizes for different levels

### Body Text
- **Readable Fonts**: Clean, professional fonts for body content
- **Excerpts**: Short text previews with "Read More" links

### Labels & Tags
- **Category Tags**: Small labels for categories (e.g., "Season", "League")
- **Badges**: Sale, New, Sold badges on products

## Color Scheme

### Primary Colors
- **Sports Theme Colors**: Athletic color palette (likely reds, blues, whites)
- **High Contrast**: Good contrast for readability
- **Professional**: Muted, professional tones

### Accent Colors
- **Call-to-Action**: Prominent buttons (likely red or primary brand color)
- **Sale Indicators**: Red for sale prices/badges
- **New Indicators**: Green or blue for new items

## UI Components

### Buttons
- **Primary Buttons**: "GET TICKETS", "Get Tickets", "Watch Stream"
- **Secondary Buttons**: "Read More", "Contact Us", "View Profile"
- **Action Buttons**: "Add to cart" for products
- **Style**: Rounded or rectangular, clear and prominent

### Cards
- **Match Cards**: Clean white/light cards with team info
- **Product Cards**: Product images with pricing and actions
- **Blog Cards**: Image, title, excerpt, read more
- **Player Cards**: Large featured player cards

### Badges & Labels
- **Sale Badge**: Red or prominent color
- **New Badge**: Green or blue
- **Sold Badge**: Gray or muted
- **Category Tags**: Small, subtle labels

### Icons
- **Social Media Icons**: Instagram, Facebook, etc.
- **Feature Icons**: Icons for services/features
- **Navigation Icons**: Menu icons, arrows

## Visual Elements

### Images
- **High-Quality Sports Imagery**: Professional photos of athletes, stadiums, equipment
- **Team Logos**: Clean logo presentations
- **Product Images**: Clear product photography
- **Background Images**: Full-width hero backgrounds

### Spacing
- **Generous White Space**: Clean, uncluttered layouts
- **Consistent Padding**: Regular spacing between elements
- **Grid Alignment**: Well-aligned grid layouts

### Animations & Interactions
- **Hover Effects**: Interactive elements on hover
- **Smooth Transitions**: Professional transitions between states
- **Carousel/Slider**: Rotating hero sections

## Content Patterns

### Match/Game Presentation
- Team vs Team format
- Venue information
- Multiple action options (tickets, stream)
- Visual team representation

### Product Presentation
- Clear product images
- Brand and product name
- Pricing with sale indicators
- Quick add-to-cart functionality

### Blog/News Presentation
- Featured image
- Category/tag
- Title and excerpt
- Read more link

### Player/Team Presentation
- Large featured image
- Position/role
- Name and details
- Biography/description
- Profile link

## Responsive Design

- **Mobile-Friendly**: Layout adapts to different screen sizes
- **Grid Flexibility**: Cards and sections stack appropriately
- **Touch-Friendly**: Buttons and links sized for mobile interaction

## Key Takeaways for PlayerStall

1. **Clean, Professional Layout**: Use generous white space and clear hierarchy
2. **Strong Visual Elements**: High-quality images and clear typography
3. **Clear Call-to-Actions**: Prominent buttons for key actions
4. **Card-Based Layouts**: Use cards for products, services, and content
5. **Section Titles**: Clear, uppercase section headers
6. **Multi-Column Footer**: Comprehensive footer with multiple sections
7. **Social Integration**: Instagram and social media presence
8. **Product Presentation**: Clear product cards with pricing and actions
9. **Blog/Content Grid**: Grid layout for blog posts and articles
10. **Athletic Aesthetic**: Sports-oriented imagery and design elements
