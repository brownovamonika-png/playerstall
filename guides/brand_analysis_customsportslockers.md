## UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITIONS

### 1. **Wood vs. Metal Positioning Strategy**

**Core Message**: Wood lockers are superior to metal in every meaningful way

**Key Arguments:**
- **Durability**: Wood outlasts metal when properly cared for
- **Aesthetics**: Professional, prestigious appearance
- **Prestige**: Creates elite, professional atmosphere
- **Warmth**: Personal, welcoming environment vs. cold industrial metal
- **Customization**: Unlimited design options vs. cookie-cutter metal units

**Competitive Differentiation:**
- Directly challenges industry standard (metal lockers)
- Positions as "comeback" trend (modern teams returning to wood)
- Uses emotional language: "cold, dented metal lockers" vs. "warm, prestigious wood"

**Evidence Points:**
- 15+ year durability examples
- Recruiting impact stories
- Team culture transformation narratives

## KEY TAKEAWAYS

### Industry Understanding
- **Primary Market**: Collegiate athletic programs seeking recruiting advantages and culture-building solutions

### Value Propositions
- **Core**: Wood quality + competitive pricing + customization + lifetime guarantee
- **Emotional**: Culture building, team identity, recruiting tool
- **Functional**: Durability, modular design, sport-specific solutions
- **Service**: Free design, family business commitment, made in Canada

### Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Authentic, personal, passionate, expert, relationship-focused
- **Style**: Story-driven, direct, educational, emotionally connected
- **Personality**: Family business warmth with professional expertise
- **Communication**: First-person storytelling, customer success focus, transformation narratives
