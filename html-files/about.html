Our Story

For over three decades, PlayerStall has been the trusted name in custom wood sports lockers. What started as a family business focused on quality and craftsmanship has grown into a company serving collegiate teams, professional organizations, and athletes across North America.

We're still here because our quality and value are unmatched. While others have come and gone, we've remained committed to one thing: making the highest quality, most affordable wood sports lockers in the industry.

Our sole focus is wood sports lockers—and that's it. This specialization means we've mastered every aspect of wood locker design, construction, and customization. We don't make metal lockers, plastic lockers, or general storage. We make wood sports lockers, and we make them better than anyone else.

Why We Choose Wood

After 30+ years in the business, we've seen it all. We've worked with metal lockers, plastic lockers, and every material in between. But we've always come back to wood—and here's why.

Durability That Lasts

When properly cared for, wood outlasts metal. We have lockers in use for 15+ years that still look professional. Metal dents, rusts, and shows wear quickly.

Professional Appearance

Wood creates a prestigious, professional atmosphere that metal simply can't match. It transforms locker rooms from storage spaces into culture-building environments.

Natural Comfort

Wood provides natural warmth and breathability. Metal lockers are cold, noisy, and create an industrial feel that doesn't inspire athletes.

Unlimited Customization

Wood allows for unlimited design options—team colors, logos, player names. Metal lockers are cookie-cutter units with limited customization.
