Locker Room Installation Guide: What to Expect and How to Prepare

Complete guide to locker room installation. Learn what to expect, how to prepare, timeline planning, and ensuring a successful installation that minimizes disruption to your program.

Introduction: Planning for Success

Proper installation planning is the key to a successful locker room project. After 30+ years and hundreds of installations, we've learned what makes installations smooth and what causes problems.

This guide walks you through everything you need to know about locker room installation, from preparation to completion.

Timeline Planning

Understanding the timeline helps you plan effectively:

Custom Manufacturing Time

Custom wood lockers require manufacturing time:

  • Standard orders: 4-6 weeks
  • Custom designs: 6-8 weeks
  • Large orders: 8-10 weeks
  • Peak season: May add 2-4 weeks

Plan ahead: Start planning 3-4 months before your desired completion date.

Installation Duration

Installation time depends on project size:

  • Small (10-20 lockers): 1-2 days
  • Medium (20-50 lockers): 3-5 days
  • Large (50-100 lockers): 1-2 weeks
  • Very large (100+ lockers): 2-3 weeks

Pre-Installation Preparation

Proper preparation prevents problems:

Space Preparation

  • Clear the space completely: Remove all existing lockers and furniture
  • Remove old lockers: Coordinate removal before installation
  • Clean the area: Ensure clean, ready space
  • Check access routes: Ensure clear paths for delivery

Infrastructure Checks

  • Electrical: Ensure adequate power if needed
  • Lighting: Verify lighting is adequate
  • Ventilation: Check HVAC systems
  • Flooring: Ensure floor is ready

Coordination

  • Schedule with other contractors: Coordinate timing
  • Notify stakeholders: Coaches, athletes, facilities
  • Plan for disruption: Minimize impact on programs
  • Arrange access: Ensure installers have access

What to Expect During Installation

Day 1: Delivery and Setup

  • Lockers delivered to site
  • Installation team arrives
  • Layout verification
  • Beginning of installation

Installation Process

  • Positioning and leveling
  • Securing to walls/floors
  • Installing hardware
  • Testing all components
  • Final adjustments

Final Day: Completion

  • Final inspection
  • Testing all locks and mechanisms
  • Cleaning and touch-ups
  • Walkthrough with facility staff
  • Handover and documentation

Minimizing Disruption

Plan installation to minimize impact:

  • Schedule during off-season: Best time for installation
  • Phased installation: Install in sections if possible
  • Quick turnaround: Our efficient process minimizes downtime
  • Clear communication: Keep everyone informed

Quality Checks

During installation, verify:

  • Proper alignment: Lockers are level and aligned
  • Hardware function: All locks and mechanisms work
  • Customization accuracy: Colors, logos, names are correct
  • Finish quality: No damage or defects
  • Complete installation: All components installed

Post-Installation

After installation is complete:

  • Final walkthrough: Review everything together
  • Documentation: Receive warranty and care information
  • Training: Learn maintenance basics
  • Support contact: Know who to call for questions

Common Installation Questions

Can installation happen during the season?

It's possible but not ideal. We recommend off-season installation when possible. If installation must happen during season, we can work around schedules.

Do we need to be present during installation?

You don't need to be present the entire time, but having someone available for questions is helpful. We'll coordinate with you.

What if something doesn't fit?

Our pre-installation measurements prevent this, but if issues arise, we'll address them immediately. Our modular design makes adjustments easy.

Conclusion: Smooth Installation

Proper planning and preparation ensure smooth installation. By following this guide and working with our team, your installation will be efficient and successful.

Our experienced installation teams have completed hundreds of projects. We know how to minimize disruption and deliver quality results.
