Complete Guide to Customizing Your Sports Lockers

Learn about seat options, lock systems, interior hardware, and creating the perfect custom locker for your team. Everything you need to know about locker customization.

Why Customization Matters

Custom lockers aren't just about looks—they're about creating a space that serves your team's specific needs. After 30+ years, we've learned that the right customization can transform a locker room from functional to exceptional.

This guide covers every customization option available, helping you create the perfect locker for your program.

Seat + Base Options

The seat/base is one of the most important customization choices. Here are your options:

Lift Up Seat with Vent Slots

Perfect for programs that need extra storage and ventilation:

  • Lift-up design provides additional storage space
  • Ventilation slots keep equipment dry
  • Ideal for wet equipment sports
  • Optional Black ABS liner available

Lift Up Seat with Vent Slots & Logo

Same functionality with team branding:

  • All benefits of vent slots
  • Team logo integrated into design
  • Creates cohesive branding
  • Optional Black ABS liner available

Lift Up Seat with Ventilation Grill

Maximum ventilation for equipment-heavy sports:

  • Perforated grill pattern for airflow
  • Black & Aluminum grill options
  • Best for hockey and football
  • Also available with logo option

Fixed Seat with Open Cubby

Simple, functional design:

  • Open cubby for easy access
  • Fixed seat design
  • Cost-effective option
  • Perfect for lighter equipment sports

Lock Options

Security is important. Choose the right lock system for your needs:

Keyless 1

Electronic keypad lock system:

  • Push-button keypad
  • No keys to lose
  • Easy code changes
  • Modern security solution

Keyless 360

Rotating dial combination lock:

  • Traditional combination lock feel
  • No batteries required
  • Reliable mechanical system
  • Easy to use

Keyless H3

Compact cylindrical lock:

  • Small, unobtrusive design
  • Keyless convenience
  • Modern appearance
  • Secure protection

Interior Hardware

Customize the interior to match your storage needs:

Coat Rod

Polished metal rod for hanging:

  • Hang uniforms and jackets
  • Keeps items organized
  • Prevents wrinkling
  • Standard in most configurations

Wall Hook

J-shaped hook for additional hanging:

  • Extra hanging space
  • Perfect for bags and accessories
  • Flexible placement
  • Multiple hooks available

Name Plate Holder

Frame for player nameplates:

  • Professional appearance
  • Easy name updates
  • Customizable design
  • Creates personal connection

Additional Customization Options

LED Lighting

Optional LED lights available for:

  • Top of locker
  • Interior illumination
  • Enhanced visibility
  • Professional appearance

Team Branding

Complete customization with:

  • Team colors
  • Logos and graphics
  • Player names and numbers
  • Custom designs

Choosing the Right Combination

Work with our design team to choose the perfect combination of options for your program. Consider:

  • Your sport's equipment needs
  • Ventilation requirements
  • Security preferences
  • Budget constraints
  • Branding goals

Conclusion: Create Your Perfect Locker

With so many customization options, you can create lockers that perfectly match your program's needs. Our free custom design consultation helps you choose the right combination.
