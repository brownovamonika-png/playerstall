## What Makes Sports Lockers "Custom"?

Standard lockers come in fixed dimensions with limited color options. Custom sports lockers are built to your exact specifications:

- **Exact Dimensions:** Width, height, and depth tailored to your space
- **Team Colors:** Exact color matching (unlimited colors)
- **Integrated Branding:** Team logos, player names, graphics built into the finish
- **Sport-Specific Layouts:** Interior configurations designed for specific equipment
- **Custom Features:** Specialized hooks, shelves, compartments
- **Wood Species:** Choice of maple, oak, cherry, or other premium finishes

Custom lockers solve problems that standard lockers cannot: space optimization, equipment accommodation, brand expression, and 15-20+ year lifespan with modular repair capability.

## Design Options & Customization

**Team Branding:** Exact color matching, integrated logos (not decals), player names engraved, custom graphics, team mottos. All branding is permanent in the wood finish.

**Interior Layout:** Adjustable shelving, specialized hooks, ventilated compartments, secure storage, equipment-specific design.

## Sport-Specific Considerations

- **Football:** Helmet storage, pad accommodation, cleat storage, 18" or 24" width

- **Basketball:** Shoe storage, minimal equipment, 12" or 15" width, clean design

## Custom Sports Lockers: Frequently Asked Questions

### What makes a sports locker "custom" versus standard?

Custom sports lockers are built to your exact specifications: dimensions, colors, branding, interior layout, and features. Standard lockers come in fixed sizes and limited color options. Custom lockers allow unlimited team color matching, integrated logos, player names, and sport-specific storage configurations that standard lockers cannot provide.

### How much do custom sports lockers cost?

Custom wood sports lockers range from $349-$599 per locker depending on tier (Semi Pro $349, Varsity $449, Pro $549, Curve $649, Stadium $599). Customization options like team colors, logos, and player names are included. Installation and shipping are additional. Most programs budget $400-$700 per locker including installation for a complete custom solution.

### How long does it take to get custom sports lockers?

Custom orders are built in approximately three months from deposit. Quick ship lockers (24" width, one signature color per style) ship in one month. For fully custom projects, we recommend starting the process 4-5 months before your target completion date to allow for design consultation and installation. 50% deposit required to begin production.

### Can custom lockers accommodate different sports equipment?

Absolutely. Custom lockers can be designed for any sport: football (helmets, pads, cleats), hockey (skates, sticks, pads), basketball (shoes, uniforms), baseball (bats, gloves, cleats), and more. Interior layouts include adjustable shelving, specialized hooks, ventilated compartments, and sport-specific storage solutions that standard lockers cannot provide.

### What customization options are available for team branding?

Custom sports lockers offer unlimited branding options: exact team color matching, integrated team logos (not decals), player names engraved or routed, custom graphics and patterns, team mottos or slogans, and hardware finishes. All branding is permanent and integrated into the wood finish, not applied as stickers or decals that can peel or fade.

### Do custom lockers require special maintenance?

Custom wood lockers require minimal maintenance: standard cleaning with mild cleaner, annual visual inspection, and optional refinishing every 10-15 years. The modular design allows component-level repairs (replace individual doors or shelves) without replacing entire lockers. Our 5 year warranty covers manufacturing defects, and proper maintenance extends lifespan to 15-20+ years.
