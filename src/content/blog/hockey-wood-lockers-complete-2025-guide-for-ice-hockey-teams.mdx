## Why Wood Sports Lockers for Hockey?

				
					Hockey facilities present unique challenges that make wood sports lockers the superior choice for ice hockey programs. Unlike other sports, hockey equipment is exposed to extreme moisture conditions from wet ice, sweat, and equipment that never fully dries between uses. Our custom wood sports lockers are specifically designed to handle these demanding conditions.
				
				
				
					After 30+ years of experience manufacturing athletic lockers for hockey programs, we've learned that wood naturally manages moisture better than any other material. Metal lockers rust and corrode in high-humidity environments, while plastic lockers become brittle and crack. Wood sports lockers, on the other hand, breathe naturally, allowing moisture to escape while maintaining structural integrity.
				

				
### The Science Behind Wood's Superiority

				
					Wood is a hygroscopic material, meaning it naturally absorbs and releases moisture based on environmental conditions. This property makes wood athletic lockers ideal for hockey locker rooms, where humidity levels fluctuate dramatically. Our custom sports lockers are constructed from premium hardwoods that have been specifically selected for their moisture resistance and durability in athletic environments.
				

				
					When compared to metal locker room lockers, wood lockers provide better insulation, preventing condensation that can damage expensive hockey equipment. The natural grain structure of wood also helps prevent the growth of mold and mildew, which is a common problem in poorly ventilated hockey facilities.
				

				
### Long-Term Durability in Ice Rink Environments

				
					Our custom wood sports lockers are built to withstand the rigors of ice rink environments for decades. With proper maintenance, our athletic lockers typically last 20-30 years or more, far exceeding the lifespan of metal alternatives. The lifetime guarantee we offer reflects our confidence in wood's durability and our commitment to your hockey program's long-term success.

## Hockey Equipment Storage Solutions

				
					Hockey players carry more equipment than athletes in almost any other sport, making efficient storage solutions critical for locker room design. Our custom wood sports lockers are specifically engineered to accommodate all hockey equipment while keeping it organized and easily accessible.
				

				
### Standard Hockey Equipment Requirements

				
					Each hockey player needs storage for multiple pieces of equipment:
				

					- **Skates:** Require ventilated storage to prevent moisture buildup and maintain blade integrity

					- **Helmet and Visor:** Need secure, easily accessible storage at the top of the locker

					- **Shoulder Pads:** Large, bulky items that need dedicated space

					- **Elbow Pads:** Smaller but essential protective equipment

					- **Gloves:** Require ventilation to prevent odor and deterioration

					- **Shin Guards:** Long items that need vertical storage space

					- **Jerseys and Base Layers:** Multiple sets require hanging space

					- **Sticks:** Need dedicated storage solutions (covered in detail below)

				

				
					Our custom sports lockers are designed with compartments specifically sized for each type of hockey equipment. The main body of the locker accommodates pads and jerseys, while ventilated drawers at the bottom provide ideal storage for skates. Integrated helmet storage at the top keeps protective gear easily accessible.

					Goalie equipment presents additional challenges, as it's significantly larger and more complex than player equipment. ## Ventilation and Moisture Management
