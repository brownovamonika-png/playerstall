## Why Customization Matters

Custom lockers aren't just about looks—they're about creating a space that serves your team's specific needs. After 30+ years, we've learned that the right customization can transform a locker room from functional to exceptional.

This guide covers every customization option available, helping you create the perfect locker for your program.

## Seat + Base Options

The seat/base is one of the most important customization choices:

### Lift Up Seat with Vent Slots

Perfect for programs that need extra storage and ventilation:

- Lift-up design provides additional storage space
- Ventilation slots keep equipment dry
- Ideal for wet equipment sports
- Optional Black ABS liner available

### Lift Up Seat with Vent Slots & Logo

Same functionality with team branding:

- All benefits of vent slots
- Team logo integrated into design
- Creates cohesive branding
- Optional Black ABS liner available

### Lift Up Seat with Ventilation Grill

Maximum ventilation for equipment-heavy sports:

- Perforated grill pattern for airflow
- Black & Aluminum grill options
- Best for hockey and football
- Also available with logo option

### Fixed Seat with Open Cubby

Simple, functional design:

- Open cubby for easy access
- Fixed seat design
- Cost-effective option
- Perfect for lighter equipment sports

## Lock Options

Security is important. Choose the right lock system:

### Keyless 1

Electronic keypad lock system:

- Push-button keypad
- No keys to lose
- Easy code changes
- Modern security solution

### Keyless 360

Rotating dial combination lock:

- Traditional combination lock feel
- No batteries required
- Reliable mechanical system
- Easy to use

### Keyless H3

Compact cylindrical lock:

- Small, unobtrusive design
- Keyless convenience
- Modern appearance
- Secure protection

## Interior Hardware

Customize the interior to match your storage needs:

### Coat Rod

Polished metal rod for hanging uniforms and jackets. Keeps items organized and prevents wrinkling. Standard in most configurations.

### Wall Hook

J-shaped hook for additional hanging. Extra space for bags and accessories. Multiple hooks available.

### Name Plate Holder

Frame for player nameplates. Professional appearance, easy name updates, creates personal connection.

## Additional Customization Options

### LED Lighting

Optional LED lights for top of locker or interior illumination. Enhanced visibility and professional appearance.

### Team Branding

Complete customization with team colors, logos and graphics, player names and numbers, and custom designs.

## Locker Customization FAQ

### What seat/base options are available for custom lockers?

We offer four main seat/base options: 1) Lift Up Seat with Vent Slots for extra storage and ventilation, 2) Lift Up Seat with Vent Slots & Logo for branding, 3) Lift Up Seat with Ventilation Grill for maximum airflow (perfect for hockey and football), and 4) Fixed Seat with Open Cubby for simple, cost-effective design. All lift-up options can include optional Black ABS liners.

### What lock systems are available for custom lockers?

We offer three keyless lock options: Keyless 1 (electronic keypad), Keyless 360 (rotating dial combination lock), and Keyless H3 (compact cylindrical lock). All provide secure, keyless convenience. Keyless 360 is mechanical and requires no batteries, while Keyless 1 offers modern electronic security with easy code changes.

### What interior hardware options can I customize?

Interior customization includes: Coat Rod (polished metal for hanging uniforms), Wall Hook (J-shaped hooks for bags and accessories), and Name Plate Holder (professional frames for player nameplates). You can also add LED lighting for top or interior illumination, and complete team branding with colors, logos, and player names.

### How do I choose the right customization combination?

Consider your sport's equipment needs, ventilation requirements, security preferences, budget constraints, and branding goals. Our free custom design consultation helps you choose the perfect combination. We'll assess your specific needs and recommend options that maximize functionality and value for your program.
