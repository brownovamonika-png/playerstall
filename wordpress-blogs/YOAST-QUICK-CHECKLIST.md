## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

- ❌ Keyphrase density too high (>2.5%) → Use synonyms
- ❌ Keyphrase density too low (<0.5%) → Add more instances
- ❌ Meta description >160 chars → Cut to 150-160
- ❌ SEO title >60 chars → Shorten to 50-60
- ❌ No transition words → Add to 30%+ of sentences
- ❌ 3+ consecutive same starts → Vary sentence starters
- ❌ Section >300 words without subheading → Add subheading
- ❌ No external links → Add 2-3 authoritative links
- ❌ Images without alt text → Add descriptive alt text

---

## QUICK FORMULAS

**Keyphrase Density:**
- For 3,000 words: Need 15-30 instances (0.5-1% density)
- Formula: (Instances ÷ Word Count) × 100 = Density %

**Transition Words:**
- Need: 30%+ of sentences
- Formula: (Sentences with transitions ÷ Total sentences) × 100 ≥ 30%

**Subheading Distribution:**
- Need: Subheading every 200-300 words
- Check: No section >300 words without subheading
