## How to Use in Yoast SEO

### In WordPress Yoast SEO Plugin:

1. **Focus Keyword:** `college sports lockers`

2. **Related Keyphrase:** Choose 1-2 from the related keyphrases list above
   - Recommended: `college athletic lockers` or `NCAA locker rooms`

3. **Synonyms:** Add these synonyms (Yoast will recognize them as variations):
   - athletic lockers
   - sports lockers
   - locker systems
   - custom lockers
   - team lockers

### Why These Matter:

- **Related Keyphrases:** Help Yoast understand the broader topic context
- **Synonyms:** Help Yoast recognize when you're naturally using alternative terms (which improves your SEO score)
