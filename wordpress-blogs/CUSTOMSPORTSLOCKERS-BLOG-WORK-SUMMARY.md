## Key Learnings & Best Practices

### Yoast SEO Requirements (Critical)
1. **Keyphrase Density:** 0.5-1.5% (15-30 instances for 3,000 words)
2. **Meta Description:** 150-160 characters, keyphrase 1-2 times max
3. **SEO Title:** 50-60 characters, keyphrase at beginning
4. **Transition Words:** 30%+ of sentences
5. **Subheadings:** Every 200-300 words
6. **Images:** 3-5 images, all with alt text, keyphrase in at least 1
7. **Links:** 3-5 internal, 2-3 external (authoritative sources)
8. **Keyphrase Distribution:** Even throughout, in 30-50% of subheadings

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Keyphrase over-optimization (>2.5% density)
- ❌ Meta description too long (>160 chars) or keyphrase >2 times
- ❌ SEO title too long (>60 chars)
- ❌ Missing transition words (<30%)
- ❌ Consecutive sentences starting with same word (3+)
- ❌ Sections >300 words without subheadings
- ❌ Missing external links
- ❌ Images without alt text

### Best Practices Established
- ✅ Create content with Yoast requirements from the start (Blogs 5 & 6)
- ✅ Use blog template for consistency
- ✅ Include comprehensive setup guides for each post
- ✅ Provide keyphrase guides with synonyms and related terms
- ✅ Follow consistent file naming: `blog-[number]-[title].html`
- ✅ Include 3,200+ words for comprehensive coverage
- ✅ Natural, human-written tone (not AI-generated)
