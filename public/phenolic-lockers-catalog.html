Material Specifications

Property Specification
Material Type High-Pressure Compact Laminate (HPCL)
Core Material Phenolic resin-impregnated kraft paper
Surface Finish Smooth, non-textured, color-through
Panel Thickness 1/2" (12mm) standard | 5/8" (16mm) heavy-duty
Density 1.35 g/cm³ minimum
Water Absorption <0.1% (24-hour immersion test)
Fire Rating Class A (ASTM E84)
Impact Resistance 20+ lb-ft (NEMA LD3)
Chemical Resistance Excellent (NEMA LD3 7.9)
Bacteria Resistance Passes ASTM G21 & G22
VOC Emissions CARB Phase 2 compliant
Temperature Range -40°F to 180°F (-40°C to 82°C)

Maintenance & Care

Easy Maintenance Protocol

Daily Cleaning:

  • Wipe surfaces with damp cloth and mild soap solution
  • Remove any spills or stains immediately
  • Ensure proper ventilation for interior drying

Weekly Deep Clean:

  • Use standard commercial cleaners or disinfectants
  • Clean hinges and locks with appropriate lubricants
  • Inspect doors for proper alignment

Annual Inspection:

  • Check all hardware for wear or looseness
  • Inspect panels for any damage (rare)
  • Verify locking mechanisms function properly
  • Tighten any loose fasteners
No special treatments, coatings, or sealants required • No rust prevention needed
